# Stage Playbook

## Setup

Goal: turn an unstructured search into a usable local system.

Actions:

- Complete `profile.md` enough to know the user's constraints, target roles, hired-by goal, and channel reality.
- Record whether important inbound channels are connected, manual, or unavailable.
- Import or create notes for active opportunities before inventing new sourcing work.
- Ask only for missing information that blocks the next useful action.

Outputs:

- Updated profile and target criteria.
- Initial active pipeline.
- First next-action list.

## Positioning

Goal: make the user's value legible.

Actions:

- Build a master resume source from experience, projects, skills, proof points, and stories.
- Convert vague claims into achievement bullets with scope, action, and result.
- Maintain role-specific pitches when the user targets multiple role families.
- Identify gaps or objections and prepare honest ways to handle them.

Outputs:

- Master resume content.
- Role-specific pitch variants.
- Bullet bank and story bank.

## Application

Goal: prepare a high-quality human-reviewed submission.

Actions:

- Read the target role note, `profile.md`, and `targets.md`.
- Fit-score the opportunity if it has not been scored.
- Draft tailored bullets, application answers, cover note, recruiter message, or intro ask.
- Mark the opportunity `to_apply` (still preparing) or `applied` (submitted) based on reality. The `## Application brief` card on the note tracks readiness within the `to_apply` state.

Outputs:

- Tailored materials.
- Updated job note.
- Clear next action.

## Active Pipeline

Goal: keep every live opportunity from going stale.

Actions:

- Review statuses, `last_interaction`, `deadline`, `next_action`, and `next_action_date`.
- Prioritize opportunities with deadlines, active conversations, or missing next actions.
- Surface blockers and propose the least awkward next move.
- Do not create artificial urgency for casual searches.

Outputs:

- Today/next-session action list.
- Updated statuses and reminders.
- Drafts for any comms that should move now or soon.

## Follow-up

Goal: keep communication warm, precise, and moving.

Actions:

- Match tone to the existing channel and relationship.
- Use the user's previous precedent when available.
- If no precedent exists, keep messages concise and moderately formal.
- Record the follow-up in the opportunity note after the user sends it or confirms it was sent.

Outputs:

- Follow-up draft.
- Reminder date when appropriate.
- Updated `last_interaction` and `next_action`.

## Offer

Goal: help the user make and communicate a good decision.

Actions:

- Capture offer terms, deadline, uncertainties, and user preferences.
- Compare against `profile.md` offer criteria.
- Draft clarification questions, negotiation messages, acceptance, decline, and wind-down notes.
- After acceptance, help close or archive other active opportunities gracefully.

Outputs:

- Offer comparison.
- Negotiation or decision drafts.
- Updated final statuses.
