# Target Criteria

## Must Have

- Role is plausibly attainable now.
- Work uses real skill or domain context.
- Clear next action: apply, intro, message, or interview prep.

## Strong Fit Signals

- (filled in by your agent during the resume conversation)

## Weak Fit / Skip Signals

- Senior-only roles with hard years-of-experience gates.
- Vague postings with no credible path to contact or application.
- Roles where the work is far outside your positioning.

## Fit Score Rubric

- 5: Strong fit, clear path, pursue immediately.
- 4: Good fit, worth applying or asking for an intro.
- 3: Possible, only pursue if low effort or strategic.
- 2: Weak fit, park unless new info appears.
- 1: Skip.
